# Contributing to GAMET

Thank you for your interest in making GAMET better. Here's how you can help.

## Ways to Contribute

### Share Your Experience

The most valuable contributions come from people who've used GAMET on a real project:

- What worked? What didn't?
- Which persona insights were most impactful?
- What did the system miss that you wish it had caught?

Open an issue with the `case-study` label.

### Industry Templates

GAMET generates personas from scratch, but industry-specific templates make the output richer. If you work in healthcare, fintech, e-commerce, education, construction, or logistics — your domain expertise would help everyone.

### New Agent Specializations

If you see a gap (DevOps, data engineering, marketing, customer success), propose a new agent with a PR.

### Bug Reports & Improvements

- Found a broken connection between skills? Open an issue.
- Have a better way to phrase a prompt? Submit a PR.
- Think a persona template is missing something? Tell us.

## Guidelines

- Keep the epistemic discipline — mark what's VERIFIED vs ASSUMED
- No project-specific data in templates
- No secrets, credentials, or personal information
- Test your changes by running `/gamet-init` on a fresh project
- English for all user-facing content

## Code of Conduct

Be kind. Be constructive. Remember that behind every persona is a real human whose day we're trying to make better.
